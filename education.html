Education,

Philippine Science High School-Caraga Region Campus (PSHS-CRC) is under the Department of Science and Technology (DOST) and it is the 13th Campus of Philippine Science High School System (PSHSS). The campus formally opened in June 2014. Offering education based on a world-class curriculum that emphasizes science, technology, engineering and mathematics. It also aims the development of well-rounded individuals and the integral development of each scholar.

Here in the Philippine Science High School-Caraga Region Campus (PSHS-CRC) have many activities and alternative learnings enhancing their knowledge in the STEM field. These include laboratory activities, field trips, elective course, clubs, and other assessments.

Conducting Laboratory activities, these activities help scholars to
enhance their mastery of science subject matter and expanding
their knowledge. Through these activities their understanding of the
nature of science increases, developing practical skills, and
developing their scientific reasoning abilities prepares them for
a science career in the future.

Additionally, they also have activities that enhance their public speaking skills and showcasing their talents. These activities are usually conducted by their subject teachers.
